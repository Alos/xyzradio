Welcome to XYZRadio!

The idea behind XYZRadio is to have a place where you can listen to interesting songs and share your musical DJ talen with your friends. You start out creating an account. After this, you may browse the music uploaded until now, or upload some music of your own (please take into consideration our terms of service before you do so). Once you got all the content that you need, you may create up to 3 playlists with 5 songs each. The server continually chooses someone at random and plays one of his or hers playlists. At that time all the users online will here the music matching talent of the current DJ. The users will rate your playlists and the server will take into consideration the score the next time it chooses DJ's. Have fun making playlists, and listening to other peoples creations!
